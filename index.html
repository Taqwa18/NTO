Frequently Asked Questions

~ How much will it cost?

You’d think there would be an obvious answer to this question – you pay for it so it would be yours, right? Actually, it’s not that simple because of all the elements of a website. Your developer should be able to tell you that you own the design, HTML, style sheets and source code (and the content if you have supplied it yourself).

~How will I pay you?

We wouldn’t advise choosing a developer who asks for one upfront payment; it will not give you much confidence or reassurance that they are going to a good job for you. Good web developers tend to ask for a small amount upfront and the rest on completion of the website..

~ Will I be able to change things on my website after its launch?

Your website should absolutely have a content management system so you can go in and change content after it has been launched. Different developers will use different content management systems; such as Magento, Woocommerce, Laravel, Shopify, Cake PHP, Symfony, Codeigniter and the most popular – WordPress.
